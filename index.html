Hi, I'm Rodrigo Martins, PhD candidate in Political Science at University of São Paulo.

I received a B.A. in Social Sciences and M.A. in Political Science from the University of São Paulo. I analysed the decision-making process on the Brazilian system of government carried out by the Constituent Assembly of 1987-88. Currently, my main research topic is the Brazilian Supreme Court, especially the justices' behaviour. I have also written working papers on research methods, political parties and democratic theory.

My research interests are: judicial politics, institutional design, political institutions, comparative politics and Brazilian politics.
