Hi, I'm Rodrigo Martins, PhD candidate in Political Science at University of São Paulo.

I received a B.A. in Social Sciences and M.A. in Political Science from University of São Paulo. I studied the decision-making process about government system in the brazilian Constituent Assembly of 1987-88. My currently main research topic is the brazilian Federal Supreme Court and justice's individual behaviour. I also have working papers about research methods, political parties and democracy.

My research interests are: judicial politics, institutional design, political institutions and brazilian politics.
